What is Hyzeer?

We’re disc golfers, not a big company. Hyzeer is open, privacy-first, and shaped by the people who play. No ads. No tracking. No paywalls. Just disc golf, for everyone.

Why We’re Different

Where We’re Headed

Phase 1: Data Collection (Now)
We’re building the most open disc golf database out there. Know a course? Add it. Got a disc? Share it. Let’s grow this together.
Phase 2: Live Course Data (Q3 2025)
See who’s playing, what the weather’s like, and when to throw. No more guessing.
Phase 3: Virtual Competitions (Q4 2025)
Friendly tournaments, leaderboards, and leagues—play with your crew or meet new folks, wherever you are.
Phase 4: Game Enhancements (2026+)
Game mods, achievements, and community challenges. Make every round unique.
Phase 5: Course Owner Tools (2026+)
Tools for course owners to keep things running smooth and players in the loop.
We want your ideas. Hyzeer is for disc golfers, by disc golfers. Let’s build it together.
